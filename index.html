FOUNDERS

Founded in 2018, GLACE is a event recommendation platform that suggests custom-tailored catalogues to individual users, based on user-specified criterias such as the duration of event, category of event and event reviews. GLACE uniquely leverages technology to provide a wide range of events and activities for users to choose from. Users can tap on to our group events collaboration tool to make coordinating meet-ups with friends and families easier. Our founders have a diverse range of background and expertise, and a common goal to deliver maximum value to our users through GLACE.
